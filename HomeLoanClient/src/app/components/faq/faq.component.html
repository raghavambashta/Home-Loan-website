Freequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1.Who can apply for a home loan with us?

Any Indian citizen between the ages of 18 to 75, either salaried or self-employed can apply for a Home Loan with us.

2.What does EMI mean?

An EMI is the 'Equated Monthly Installment', which is the monthly payment that you make towards repaying your Home Loan on a specified date until your loan is repaid in full.

3.What are the necessary documents required to apply for the loan?

The necessary documents for the loan application are: 1. Aadhaar Card 2. PAN Card 3. Salary Slip 4. LOA 5. NOC 6. Agreement.

The step-by-step procedure to apply for the loan is as follows:
